About Me

I was born and rasied in Orlando, Florida

(yes, I've been to Disney a lot)

I started college at the University of South Florida

I graduated a year early from high school and moved to Tampa.
I started off studying Chemical Engineering.
I spent some time volunteering in a few research labs (psychopharmacology, tissue engineering) and ultimately realized that working in a wet lab wasn't for me.
I took a MATLAB course my Sophomore year and loved coding so much that I switched my major!

I finished my Computer Science degree at the University of Central Florida

Before I started at UCF, I competed in their annual programming competition and won a spot on the Competitive Programming Team.
While at UCF, I worked at a company called CCMC as an Operations Coordinator.

I'm living in Boston and working at Systems and Technology Research

I work as a Program Manager and a Software Engineer between a few ongoing projects.
My work generally centers around prototyping cybersecurity tools.
